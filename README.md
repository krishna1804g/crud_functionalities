# legion_webhackathon

GoodWorkConnect is your platform for change. Join us in creating an impact where it matters most – in cities. We connect those who want to make a difference with opportunities that matter. Earn badges, show your impact, and be part of a vibrant community dedicated to progress. Our focus on city data comparison fuels effective change. Covering 12 out of the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals, we're driving real action. Want to see your city on top? Earn badges for your city's progress and rise to the forefront of positive change. Join us to empower, make an impact, and transform our cities together. Your actions count. Your impact matters. GoodWorkConnect – where change begins.
